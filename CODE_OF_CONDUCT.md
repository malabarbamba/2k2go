# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

To keep collaboration constructive, participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Give technical, actionable feedback.
- Keep discussions on-topic.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private/personal information.
- Threatening language, intimidation, or sustained disruption.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action needed to protect the community, including:

- Editing or removing content.
- Locking conversations.
- Restricting or removing contributor access.

## Reporting

Use GitHub's content reporting tools for conduct violations.

For severe cases that also involve a security concern, follow `SECURITY.md` for private reporting.
